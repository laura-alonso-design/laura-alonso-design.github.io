Adogtapp

UX/UI

User needs

Users who decide to adopt a dog need to know which is a good option for their lifestyle.

Strategy

After studying the user and his needs I obtained the requirements of the product. Then I started with the interaction design so that the product had the functionalities expected, trying to give it a warm and casual.




 

Which dog could be the one?

Adogtapp includes some questions about the user's lifestyle so it make some matches and recommends possible options. It also gives user the information that really care: behaviour and history.

Possibility to meet them

Users can make an appointment to meet their future dogs at the dog shelter and know each other.

Keep track the dog adaptation

The user can observe the dog's adaptation and keep updated the dog shelter.
