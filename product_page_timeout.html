Time Out

Illustration

Work required

An illustrations that represents the idea and lyrics of the song "Mossos de escuadra" (by Tony el Gitano) for the weekly Barcelona magazine Time Out

Idea

The song talks about the police repression in the gypsy neighborhood of La Mina (Barcelona). The way to represent that is an old and messy building facade with the huge shadow of the police, that represents this dreaded presence.

 

Concept

The chaos of the police presence. The building is empty but it feels restless.

Graphic Representation

All is blue, the sadness color. Using different grades of blue color the more visible elements are the police and the messy clothes of the building. The dark and huge blue mass, the police is covering the different elements of the building that represent the gypsy people.
