User Centered Design

I became a designer because I have always been fascinated by having ideas in my mind and I wanted the ability to make them be truth. I consider that I have the skill to solve user needs in a rational way, so I like to design and develope my work justifying all the decisions that I make. I trust the Cartesian Method to solve problems, scientist method is based on it so I think that is the correct way also to solve user needs.

Analysis

First I do research to learn about the user and I study the context of the design. The conclusions and insghts that I obtain in this process gives me the clues to focus the design and also the product requirements.

Synthesis

With all the information I propose concepts and develope some ideas and when I see the right way to keep going I think about how the product requirements can be translated into design language.

Verification

When I think that I have a solution I test the product so I can verify, or not, the design. I think is impossible for a product to be perfect so the verification is important to design the best product possible.
