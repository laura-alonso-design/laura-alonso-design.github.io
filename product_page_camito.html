Camito

Product Design

User needs

The users are childrem from 0 to 36 month that need to play and develope their psychomotor skills at the same time.

Strategy

I thought some suitable features for children that made possible make a relation between the child action and the toy reaction. Then I thought in a interesting and funny animal for children like a chameleon and I worked on a friendly shape for them. Finally I though some appropiate for children and their safety.

 

Intelligent Color Lights

Camito has an Arduino motherboard, so when children place the toy on a color, it lights up in that color.

Vibration Motor

When the tongue is pulled Camito moves and stimulate the child.

Sound and music

It sounds when children press Camito's head.
