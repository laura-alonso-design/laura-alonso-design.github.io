Book Cover

Illustration, Graphic Design

Work Required

A cover for a football book.

Idea

This book is an essay and thesis about football trainig and education and talks about how organize a team and a football club. The cover illustration represents the intellectualy and knowledge of this sport.

 

Concept

The reader undertands inmediatly the subject of the book.

Graphic Representation

All the book cover (front and back) is a football field with a match strategy. The main element is the ball with the eye that represents the football knowledge.
